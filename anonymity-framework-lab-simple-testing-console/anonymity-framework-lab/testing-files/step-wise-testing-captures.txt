- WireGuard UDP response from 10.0.10.26:51820 to 20.20.20.1
- ICMP tunnel test between 20.20.20.1 and 20.20.20.2

TEST 2 - VPS-1 relay test

- VPS-1 forwards traffic toward VPS-2 at 20.20.20.3
